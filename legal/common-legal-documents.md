# Common Legal Documents

## Sales

### Mutual NDA

Says neither party will disclose anything from the engagement to any other parties

### Master Service Agreement (MSA)

Contractual document that specifies the performance objects and outlines the responsibilities of both parties

### Statement of Work (SOW)

Specific to a single contract. Activities, deliverables, timelines, quote, etc.

### Rules of Engagement (RoE)

Covers specifics of testing. Find out what is in scope and what is out of scope, what kind of attacks are acceptable and what are not (DoS, Social Engineering, etc).&#x20;
